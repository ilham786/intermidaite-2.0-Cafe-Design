Our Philosophy
"We don't make coffee. We make the reason you came back." — Kenji Adeyemi, Founder

Sōru was born from a single obsession: what if the ritual mattered as much as the result? We trace every bean to its origin, every roast to its intention, every cup to the hands that will hold it.

Our baristas don't just brew — they interpret. Each drink is a translation of terroir, of craft, of the quiet conversation between farmer and drinker. We source from twelve origins, roast in-house every 48 hours, and serve nothing we wouldn't drink ourselves.

This is not a cafe. This is a reason to pause.
